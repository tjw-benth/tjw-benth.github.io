Tom Williams

Arctic Marine Ecologist / Aspiring Data Scientist

Biography

I am a third-year PGR at the University of Southampton, assessing the responses of Arctic and Antarctic biodiversity to climate change and resulting consequences for benthic ecosystem functioning.

Research Interests

I have broad interests in marine benthic ecology and data science. I am well versed in the use of experimentally manipulated environments to evaluate organism responses to disturbances and the consequences for ecosystem functioning. I have also developed trait-based computational models that explores the functional repercussions of biodiversity loss in realistic scenarios that incorporate extinction orders based on vulnerabilities and species interactions.
